Hotkeys

A smart desktop enhances workflows through shortcuts. For example, windows can be controlled by hotkeys (aka keyboard shortcuts).

  • Minimize your workspace: CTRL+ALT+DOWN ARROW
  • Reveal all Finsemble windows: CTRL+ALT+UP ARROW
  • Launch favorited items: CTRL+ALT+1, 2, 3, ...
  • Note: The apps that launch correspond to the order in which apps and workspaces are pinned to the toolbar.
  • Search: CTRL+SHIFT+F
  • Call the toolbar: CTRL+ALT+T
  • Hide the toolbar: CTRL+ALT+H

You can also minimize the workspace and reveal all Finsemble windows using the buttons on the right of the toolbar.

You can set your own hotkeys when you develop your smart desktop.
