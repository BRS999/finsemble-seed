Workspaces

Your workspace is comprised of all of your Finsemble windows on all of your monitors. When you start up your smart desktop, your workspace will reload with the exact same configuration as when you shut down.

Let's try out the workspace management capabilities.

  • On the toolbar, click the "Default Workspace" button.
  • Click the "Save As" button.
  • Name your sample workspace.
  • Press the hotkey CTRL+ALT+SHIFT+R to restart Finsemble.
  • Viola! Here we are again.

You can save a particular configuration of applications using the Workspace menu. You can restore your workspace at any time, switching between different sets of applications for different tasks.
